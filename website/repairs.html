Warning Signs

WHEN TO CALL FOR REPAIRS

Don't wait for small problems to become big expenses. These signs indicate your roof needs attention:

Water Stains

Brown spots on ceilings or walls indicate water intrusion that needs immediate attention.

Curling Shingles

Shingles that curl, buckle, or lift are compromised and leave your roof vulnerable.

Granule Loss

Finding granules in gutters signals shingle deterioration and reduced protection.

Daylight in Attic

If you can see light through your roof boards, water can get in too.

Ice Dams

Ice buildup at roof edges indicates ventilation issues that damage roofing over time.

Moss or Algae

Growth on your roof traps moisture and accelerates deterioration.

Honest Assessment

REPAIR VS. REPLACE

We'll always give you an honest recommendation based on what's best for your property and budget.

01

When Repair Makes Sense

Localized damage, roof under 15 years old, minor wear, or budget constraints. Repairs can extend roof life significantly when done properly.

02

When Replacement is Better

Widespread damage, roof over 20 years old, multiple past repairs, or major structural issues. Sometimes replacement is more cost-effective long-term.

03

Our Honest Approach

We won't sell you a new roof when repairs will do. And we won't keep patching a roof that needs replacement. We recommend what's truly best.

04
