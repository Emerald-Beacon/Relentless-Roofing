Roofing Systems

FLAT ROOFING OPTIONS

TPO Roofing

Thermoplastic Polyolefin - the most popular commercial choice. Excellent UV resistance, energy efficiency, and weldable seams. 15-30 year warranties.

EPDM Roofing

Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer - durable synthetic rubber. Proven track record, cost-effective for large roofs. 20-30 year lifespan.

Modified Bitumen

Reinforced asphalt membrane. Multi-layer system with excellent waterproofing. Torch-applied or self-adhered options.

Built-Up Roofing (BUR)

Traditional "tar and gravel" system. Multiple layers of bitumen and reinforcing fabric. Time-tested performance.

PVC Roofing

Polyvinyl Chloride membrane. Superior chemical resistance - ideal for restaurants and buildings with rooftop equipment.

Roof Coatings

Extend existing roof life with reflective coatings. Silicone, acrylic, and polyurethane options available.

Comparison

TPO VS. EPDM: WHICH IS RIGHT FOR YOU?

Choose TPO When:

  • Energy efficiency is priority
  • You want a white/reflective roof
  • Hot climate (Arizona)
  • Budget is moderate-high

Choose EPDM When:

  • Budget is primary concern
  • Large roof area to cover
  • Black color is acceptable
  • Proven long-term track record
