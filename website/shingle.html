Shingle Options

ASPHALT SHINGLE TYPES

Architectural Shingles

Also called dimensional or laminate shingles. Multi-layer construction creates depth and shadow for a premium look. 30-50 year warranties.

3-Tab Shingles

Traditional single-layer shingles. Budget-friendly option with clean, uniform appearance. 20-25 year warranties.

Designer Shingles

Premium shingles that mimic slate, shake, or tile. Highest impact resistance and longest warranties - up to lifetime.

Impact Resistant

Class 4 rated shingles built to withstand hail. May qualify for insurance discounts in storm-prone areas.

Cool Roof Shingles

Reflective granules reduce heat absorption. Lower energy bills and more comfortable homes in hot climates.

Algae Resistant

Copper-infused granules prevent black streaks from algae growth. Keep your roof looking clean longer.
